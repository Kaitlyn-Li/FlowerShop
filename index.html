About Us

Best Flowers in Town

the studio

Flower. is a natural floral design studio specializing in weddings and exclusive events in the greater Seattle area. We believe the best designs are rooted in place and time, and they should be a reflection of the season and the environment we’re working in. Our aesthetic is inspired by the natural colors, organic shapes, and carefree movement found all around us in beautiful fields and gardens.

Learn More

the designer

Sarah Baileys is the lead floral designer and owner of Flower. She began this business after moving to Washington and falling in love with the incredible bounty of flowers the PNW has to offer.

She has a BA in Literature and worked in the publication industry for many years before deciding she wanted to get out from behind the desk and create with her hands. Floral design has since become an outlet, a way to communicate the beauty she sees in the world around her.

Sarah looks to nature for inspiration and is known for creating artistic designs and stunning installations with flowers and foliage that look like they might have organically grown in the area surrounding your wedding venue. As a long time gardener, she takes pride in sourcing the freshest, most local, and highest quality blooms possible for your day.

Our Services

a note from Sarah

Thank you for your interest in beautiful florals for your wedding day! Our studio specializes in creating artistic designs led by the changing seasons that help deepen our connection to the natural world.

When you walk through a wildflower meadow or a lush garden, notice the way blooms grow at different heights, the way they arch and bend towards the light to create a sense of airiness and movement. You’ll see vines climbing and weaving their way through neighboring plants and branches reaching out to provide a structural element to the landscape.

Witnessing this untamed beauty is where our design process begins. We’re captivated by the organic shapes, colors, and textures we observe all around us in the natural world, and we use this as inspiration to communicate what we see through our work.

We believe flowers are a gift that provides joy and delight, and that’s ultimately what we hope to provide for you. We’re glad you’re here.

services

We provides full-service floral design for weddings and exclusive events in the greater Seattle area. Full service design includes:

  • Consultation and creation of mood board or floral recipe
  • Custom estimate
  • Delivery and installation of florals at your venue
  • Personal flowers (bouquets, boutonnieres)
  • Ceremony flowers (aisle arrangements, altar arrangements, floral arches, or chuppahs)
  • Reception flowers (table centerpieces, cocktail bud vases, bar arrangements)
  • Collection of hard goods and remaining florals at end of celebration to be composted responsibly

pricing

Floral design services for weddings and events begin at $4,000.

availability

We offer floral design services during the months of March-November at venues within two hours of the greater Seattle area, including Woodinville, Tacoma, Whidbey Island, Kirkland, Bellevue, Snohomish, Bellingham, and more.
